Tetris Techniques

T-Spins allow for more efficient attack as well as point bonuses (x2 lines per piece).

Keeping back to back (Tetrises and T-Spins only) allows for bonuses as well.

All clears, or clearing the entire board at once, send 10 lines at once. They are the most powerful attack.

Chaining line clears, or combos, are an efficient way of downstacking.
